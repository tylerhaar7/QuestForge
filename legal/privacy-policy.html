Privacy Policy

Your Data & Your Adventure

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

QuestForge is built by adventurers, for adventurers. We take your privacy as seriously as a rogue takes their lockpicks. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how we protect it.

Section I

Information We Collect

When you use QuestForge, we collect the following types of information:

Account Information

  • Email address — used to create and authenticate your account.

Game Data

  • Character data — your created characters, including name, race, class, stats, inventory, and backstory choices.
  • Campaign state — quest progress, companion relationships, journal entries, NPC interactions, and world state.
  • Game preferences — settings such as difficulty, theme, and notification preferences.

Automatically Collected Data

  • Device information — device type and operating system version, used to ensure app compatibility.
  • Crash and performance data — anonymized error reports to help us improve the app.
Section II

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Create and manage your QuestForge account.
  • Save and synchronize your game progress across sessions.
  • Generate AI-narrated game content tailored to your campaign.
  • Improve the app through anonymized analytics and crash reporting.
  • Communicate with you about important account or service updates.
Section III

AI-Generated Content & Your Data

QuestForge uses Claude, an AI model by Anthropic, to serve as your Dungeon Master and narrate your adventure. Here is how your data interacts with the AI:

  • The AI receives only in-game context — your character details, current quest state, and recent game events — to generate narrative responses.
  • No personal information (such as your email address) is sent to the AI.
  • AI interactions are processed in real time and are not used to train AI models.
  • All AI communication is routed through our secure server infrastructure.
Section IV

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud infrastructure provider, on servers located in the United States.

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Database access is governed by row-level security policies, ensuring you can only access your own data.
  • We follow industry-standard security practices to protect your information from unauthorized access.
Section V

Data Sharing & Sale

We do not sell your personal data. Period. Your information is never shared with third-party advertisers or data brokers.

We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — trusted partners who help operate the app (e.g., cloud hosting, AI processing), bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal requirements — when required to comply with applicable law, regulation, or legal process.
Section VI

Your Rights & Account Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct any inaccurate information in your account.
  • Delete your account and all associated data. Upon request, we will permanently remove your account, characters, campaigns, and all related game data from our servers.
  • Export your data in a portable format upon request.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at questforge.app@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Section VII

Children's Privacy

QuestForge is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take prompt steps to delete it.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at questforge.app@gmail.com.

Section VIII

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or via email. Your continued use of QuestForge after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
